## Who typically buys candy in bulk

Bulk orders are common for events, corporate gifting, schools, and fundraisers. If you need large quantities for a theme or season, a bulk format is often the most efficient path.

The [American-Made Candy](/made-in-usa-candy) guide details origin standards that matter for bulk buying.

## Choose the right bulk format

The best format depends on how you will distribute the candy.

- Large bags for snack tables
- Individual portions for gift bags
- Mixed assortments for variety

Compare pack sizes on [bulk gummy bears](/bulk-gummy-bears) and evaluate volume pricing through [wholesale](/wholesale).

## Estimate quantity without guessing

A simple approach is to estimate by event type:

- Snack table: one small handful per guest
- Gift bags: one portion per recipient
- Large events: add 10 percent extra for refills

## Plan storage and delivery timing

Bulk orders require a little extra planning. Consider where the candy will be stored and how far in advance you need it. Order early enough to allow for shipping and event prep.
