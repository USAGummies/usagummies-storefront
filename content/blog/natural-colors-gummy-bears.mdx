## The Problem with Artificial Dyes

Artificial food dyes like Red 40 have been linked to:
- **Hyperactivity in children** (studies show increased ADHD symptoms)
- **Allergic reactions** (hives, asthma, skin irritation)
- **Long-term health concerns** (potential carcinogenic effects in animal studies)

The **FDA requires warning labels** for artificial dyes in some countries, but not in the U.S.—leaving parents to navigate the risks on their own.
