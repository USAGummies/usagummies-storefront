### Section outline + key copy blocks
**1) What the warnings actually say**
Regulators reported major mismatches between labeled and actual melatonin content in unregistered gummies, with some products exceeding labeled amounts by several hundred percent. This variability creates dosing uncertainty and safety risk.

**2) Why label variability matters**
Small changes in melatonin dose can change how you feel the next morning. If the label can’t be trusted, consistent results aren’t possible.

**3) What “melatonin‑free” really means (and why it matters)**
Melatonin‑free does not mean “sleep‑free.” Magnesium glycinate supports relaxation through magnesium + glycine—without adding a hormone that may be variable or over‑dosed.

**4) How to choose a safer sleep gummy**
- Choose brands that publish third‑party testing or Certificates of Analysis.
- Avoid products with unclear serving sizes or ambiguous “per serving” claims.
- Start with non‑hormonal supports if you’re sensitive to next‑day grogginess.
