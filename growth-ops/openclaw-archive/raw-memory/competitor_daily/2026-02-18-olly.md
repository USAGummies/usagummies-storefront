## Key Findings
1. **Product Claims & Gaps:**
   - **Strengths:**
     - **Probiotics + multivitamin combo** (digestive support + vitamins A, D, C, E, B, Zinc).
     - **Marketing language:** "No synthetic flavors or colors," "gluten-free," "non-GMO."
     - **Flavor:** "Blissful Berry" (single flavor, but positioned as universally loved).
   - **Weaknesses:**
     - **No "Made in USA" claim** (manufacturing location undisclosed).
     - **Probiotic strain not specified** (just "probiotics"—parents prefer transparency).
     - **Price:** ~$18.99 for 70-count (35-day supply)—**higher cost per serving** than USA Gummies.

2. **Messaging Opportunity:**
   - OLLY **lacks transparency** about:
     - **Where the gummies are made** (USA Gummies can highlight **USA manufacturing**).
     - **Probiotic details** (USA Gummies could specify strains if applicable, or emphasize **no probiotics = simpler formula**).
   - Their **"no synthetic colors/flavors" claim** is vague (USA Gummies can specify **"fruit/vegetable-based colors"**).

Plus, our **simpler formula** skips probiotics (which many kids don’t need) for a **cleaner, more affordable** daily vitamin!
