# Competitor Intel Brief: SmartyPants Kids Multivitamin Gummies (B004QQ9LVS)
**Date:** 2026-02-18
**Time:** 10:52 AM PST

## Key Findings
1. **Product Claims & Gaps:**
   - **Strengths:**
     - Highlights **Omega-3 (EPA/DHA)** and **immune support** (Vitamins D3, C, Zinc).
     - **Gluten-free**, **3 fruit flavors**, **120-count (30-day supply)**.
   - **Weaknesses:**
     - **No explicit "Made in USA" claim** (manufacturing location unclear).
     - **No mention of natural colors/flavors** (uses "natural flavors" but not detailed).
     - **Price:** ~$29.99 for 120-count ($1/day) vs. USA Gummies’ **lower cost per serving**.

2. **Messaging Opportunity:**
   - SmartyPants **lacks transparency** about:
     - **Where the gummies are made** (parents prefer USA-made for safety).
     - **Source of "natural flavors"** (USA Gummies can highlight **fruit/vegetable extracts**).
   - Their **Omega-3 focus** is strong, but they **don’t emphasize simplicity** (USA Gummies: fewer additives).

3. **Immediate Action Recommendation:**
   - **Amazon Listing Update:** Add a **comparison bullet point** to USA Gummies’ listing:
     > "✅ **Simpler & Safer:** Made in the USA with **real fruit/vegetable colors** (no synthetic dyes) and **no mystery ‘natural flavors’**—just clean, traceable ingredients. Compare to brands that hide manufacturing locations!"
   - **GBP/Website FAQ:** Add:
     > **Q:** "How do your gummies compare to SmartyPants?"
     > **A:** "We’re proudly **made in the USA** (they don’t disclose theirs) and use **real fruit/vegetable colors** (no vague ‘natural flavors’). Plus, we’re **more affordable per serving**—same great nutrients, simpler ingredients!"
