# Competitor Daily Intelligence - 2026-02-14

## Top 5 Amazon Gummy Bear Brands
1. Haribo
   - Consistently ranked as one of the best gummy bear brands
   - Known for toothsome texture and well-rounded flavor
   - Popular varieties include Haribo Watermelon
2. Albanese
   - Offers a variety of flavors and colors
   - Known for larger size and softer texture
   - Provides a good level of chew
3. SmartSweets
   - Fruity gummy bears with flavors like raspberry, apple, and peach
   - Priced at $5.99 per 1.5-ounce bag
4. YumEarth
   - Organic gummy bears
   - Allergy-friendly and non-GMO
   - No artificial dyes
5. Walmart (Great Value)
   - Affordable option
   - Comparable to national brands in taste and texture

## Competitor Analysis
- Haribo dominates the market with its strong brand recognition and consistent quality
- Albanese offers a good alternative with a variety of flavors and textures
- SmartSweets appeals to health-conscious consumers with its fruit-flavored, lower-sugar options
- YumEarth caters to the organic and allergy-friendly market
- Walmart's Great Value brand provides an affordable option for budget-conscious consumers

## Pricing Comparison
- Haribo: $2.99 - $5.99 per bag
- Albanese: $3.49 - $6.99 per bag
- SmartSweets: $5.99 per 1.5-ounce bag
- YumEarth: $3.99 - $5.99 per bag
- Walmart (Great Value): $1.99 - $3.49 per bag
