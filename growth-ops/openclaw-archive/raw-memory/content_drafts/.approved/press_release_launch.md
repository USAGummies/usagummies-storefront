## Press Release Draft: "American Gummy Bear Brand Proves Premium Candy Can Be Made Without Artificial Dyes"

**FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASE**

**USA Gummies Launches, Proving Premium Candy Can Be Made Without Artificial Dyes**

**PHILADELPHIA, PA – [Date]** – USA Gummies, a new American-made premium gummy bear brand, today announced its official launch, offering consumers a delicious alternative to traditionally artificially colored candies. Proudly sourced, manufactured, and packed entirely in FDA-registered facilities across the United States, USA Gummies are colored naturally with real fruit and vegetable extracts, completely free from artificial dyes.

Founded by two lifelong friends from Philadelphia, USA Gummies embodies a simple, honest approach to candy making. “We started USA Gummies because we wanted to make candy you can hand to your kids without second-guessing,” says the founders. “It’s about bringing back that classic gummy bear flavor and texture, but doing it right – with natural colors and supporting American jobs along the way. That matters to us.”

USA Gummies are characterized by their soft, chewy texture and all-natural fruit flavors, including Cherry, Lemon, Green Apple, Orange, and Watermelon. The brand emphasizes transparency and quality, ensuring a premium taste experience without the harsh aftertaste often associated with artificial ingredients.

With a strong emphasis on American manufacturing, USA Gummies not only delivers a high-quality product but also contributes to the national economy. The brand has already garnered a 4.8-star rating from verified Amazon buyers, a testament to its commitment to customer satisfaction.

USA Gummies are available for purchase directly from their website, www.usagummies.com, where customers can enjoy free shipping on orders of 5 bags or more. The product is also available on Amazon at https://www.amazon.com/USA-Gummies-American-Artificial-Natural/dp/B0G1JK92TJ.
