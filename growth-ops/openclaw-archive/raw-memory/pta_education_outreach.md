# PTA & School Health Education Outreach (W12)

## STRATEGY
Email PTA groups, school wellness committees, and parent organizations with educational content about artificial dyes in children's candy. Position USA Gummies as the trusted, all-natural alternative. This is NOT a hard sell — it's education-first marketing that builds brand awareness and creates positive association.

## KEY MESSAGING
- **FDA allows dyes banned in Europe**: Red 40, Yellow 5, Yellow 6, Blue 1 are in most gummy bears
- **Studies link artificial dyes to hyperactivity**: Multiple studies (Southampton study, FDA review) show connection between artificial food dyes and behavioral issues in children
- **"Always Been Dye-Free"**: USA Gummies was founded specifically to offer gummy bears without artificial dyes — we didn't reformulate, we started clean
- **Colors from real food**: Our colors come from fruits and vegetables (beet juice, turmeric, spirulina, etc.)
- **100% Made in USA**: Every ingredient sourced, manufactured, and packed domestically

## HOW TO FIND PTA CONTACTS
1. Web search: "{city} PTA email" or "{school district} PTA contact"
2. Search: "site:pta.org {state} local PTA email"
3. Look for PTA newsletters with contact info
4. School district websites often list PTA contacts
5. Target states with recent dye-free legislation (California, etc.)

## PRIORITY STATES (active or recent dye legislation)
- California (banned Red 3 in 2023)
- New York (bills introduced)
- Illinois, Washington, New Jersey (proposed bans)
- Any state with active food safety advocacy
