**USA Gummies Launches All-American Gummy Bears: Premium, All-Natural Gummy Bears Made Entirely in the USA**

*February 19, 2026 — USA Gummies, a new American confectionery brand, today announced the availability of its flagship product, All American Gummy Bears — premium gummy bears made with all-natural flavors and colors sourced from real fruits and vegetables, manufactured, packed, and sold entirely in the United States.*

---

In a market dominated by imported candy and artificial ingredients, USA Gummies is taking a different approach. Every bag of All American Gummy Bears is sourced, manufactured, and packed in the USA, supporting American jobs and delivering a cleaner ingredient list that today's health-conscious consumers demand.

"We saw an opportunity to give Americans a gummy bear they can feel good about," said Ben, Founder of USA Gummies. "No artificial dyes, no imported mystery ingredients — just great-tasting gummy bears made right here at home."

- **100% Made in the USA** — sourced, manufactured, and packed domestically

- **5 Classic Flavors** — Cherry, Lemon, Green Apple, Orange, and Watermelon
- **7.5 oz bags** starting at $5.99
