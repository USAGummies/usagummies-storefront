## PIN TEMPLATES

### Pin Type 1: Blog Post Pins (tall image + title)
**Size:** 1000 x 1500px (2:3 ratio â€” Pinterest optimal)
**Format:**
- Top 40%: Product photo or lifestyle image
- Bottom 60%: Solid cream/navy background with title text
- Title text: Bold, 3-5 words max per line
- Subtitle: 1 line, smaller text
- Logo/URL at bottom: usagummies.com

**Design specs:**
- Background: `#f8f5ef` (cream) or `#1B2A4A` (navy)
- Title text: `#1B2A4A` (navy on cream) or `#ffffff` (white on navy)
- Accent: `#c7362c` (red) for CTAs or highlights
- Font: Clean sans-serif (Space Grotesk or similar)

### Pin Type 2: Infographic Pins (educational)
**Size:** 1000 x 2100px (taller â€” more real estate)
**Format:**
- Title at top
- 3-5 facts/steps with icons
- CTA at bottom
- Brand logo

### Pin Type 3: Product Pins (direct link to shop)
**Size:** 1000 x 1500px
**Format:**
- Clean product photo on cream background
- "Dye-Free Gummy Bears" text overlay

## SEO NOTES

Every pin needs:
1. **Title:** Include primary keyword naturally (max 100 chars)
2. **Description:** 2-3 sentences with keywords + 3-5 hashtags (max 500 chars)
3. **Alt text:** Describe the image naturally with keywords
4. **Link:** Full URL with UTM params: `?utm_source=pinterest&utm_medium=pin&utm_campaign=dyefree`
5. **Board:** Pin to most relevant board first, then add to 1-2 secondary boards
