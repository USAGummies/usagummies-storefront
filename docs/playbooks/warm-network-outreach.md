**Goal:** 5–10 paid orders by 9pm PT today

## How to use

- Pick 30–100 people from iMessage / IG DMs / Facebook / phone contacts / family / military community
- Send the script that fits the channel (text vs DM vs email)
- **Do not blast.** One-to-one. Personalize the first line if you can ("hey [name]" beats "hey").
- Don't repeat-send. One ask per person.
- Don't follow up if they don't reply. Permission marketing — no chase.

## Who to message FIRST (priority order)

1. **Repeat customers** — anyone who ordered twice (e.g. ohiojud@hotmail.com)
2. **Recent DTC customers (last 90 days)** — already converted once, low friction to come back
3. **Family + close friends** — they'll respond fastest
4. **Military / veteran community** — strongest doctrine match (3-state veteran-owned facility story)
5. **Patriotic-parent friends** — the SVA-1 archetype, real or extended network
6. **Wholesale prospects you've talked to** — "we're now shipping; want a sample?"

## What NOT to say

- Don't lead with founder identity ("I made this")
- Don't apologize for asking
- Don't oversell — let the bag do the work
- Don't push the bundle ("you should buy 5") — let them pick
- Don't ask for a public review — just an honest reaction in private
