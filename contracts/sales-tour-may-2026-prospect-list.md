## Methodology (locked from Viktor 2026-04-29)

For every prospect on this list:

1. **Company-first deep research** — official site, LinkedIn company page, press releases, GetYourGuide / TripAdvisor supplier listings, BBB filings, vendor partner pages, industry news.
2. **Identify named buyer** — read org charts, job postings, supplier pages. Cross-reference Prospeo / LeadIQ / SignalHire / RocketReach (different surfaces find different people).
3. **Verify email pattern** — Prospeo shows confirmed format with confidence %; cross-check against publicly listed emails.
4. **Personalize** — reference real context (their current contract, recent press, specific location, vendor policy language).
5. **HubSpot before send** — contact + deal + research notes logged before email goes out.
6. **Hand to Viktor** for verification when the format guess isn't confirmed.

**No more shots in the dark.** No bouncing on inferred email formats. No emailing a generic info@ as a fallback.
