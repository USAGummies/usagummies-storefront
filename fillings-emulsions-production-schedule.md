## PRODUCTION PROCESS GROUPS

Items are organized by **shared base doughs/batters/processes** to maximize efficiency.

### Group A — LAMINATED DOUGH (2-day process)
**Shared base:** Croissant dough (détrempe + butter block, 3 folds)
- Butter Croissant, Almond Croissant, Pain Au Chocolat, Ham & Cheese Everything, Ham Swiss Egg, Sausage Provolone Egg, Spicy Potato Cheddar Egg, Strawberries & Cream, Morning Bun, Croque Croissant

### Group B — BRIOCHE LAMINATED DOUGH (2-day process)
**Shared base:** Brioche dough (enriched) + butter lamination
- Kouign Amann, Guava Cream Cheese Brioche, French Apple Brioche, King's Cake

### Group C — GUAVA PASTELES DOUGH
**Shared base:** Puff pastry / laminated dough + guava paste + cream cheese filling
- Guava Pasteles (single, half dozen, dozen)

### Group D — MACARON SHELLS (same-day process, fillings vary)
**Shared base:** French or Italian meringue + almond flour
- All 12+ flavors — piped in batches, filled with flavor-specific ganaches/buttercreams/curds
- **Fillings made in advance (1–3 day shelf life refrigerated)**

### Group E — TROMPE L'OEIL DESSERTS (multi-component, 2–3 day process)
**Sub-processes:**
- **Mousse bases** (fruit purée + gelatin + whipped cream)
- **Insert cores** (compote, curd, crémeux, cheesecake base)
- **Sponge/cake bases** (biscuit joconde, chocolate cake, etc.)
- **Spray/glaze** (cocoa butter + color, mirror glaze)
- **Molding** (silicone molds, freeze, unmold, spray)

### Group F — BREAD DOUGHS (same-day, long ferment)
**Sub-groups:**
- French baguette dough (poolish → autolyse → bulk → shape → proof → bake)
- Demi baguette dough (same base + fillings added before final proof)
- Sourdough (levain build → autolyse → bulk → cold retard overnight → bake) — Fri/Sat only

### Group G — QUICK BREADS (same-day)
- Scone dough (cold butter method, cut, bake)
- Crumb cake batter + streusel topping

### Group H — MEAT PIE PRODUCTION
**Shared base:** Pie dough (shortcrust or similar)
**Fillings** (cooked in advance):
- Picadillo (traditional Cuban), Chicken, Cuban Sandwich (ham/pork/swiss/pickle), Ropa Vieja
- Shape, fill, egg wash, bake

## CRITICAL PATH ITEMS — DO NOT SKIP

1. **Trompe l'Oeil mousse must be made 2 days before service** — freeze time required
2. **Laminated dough needs 3 folds + overnight retard** — minimum 18-hour lead time
3. **Macaron shells need overnight maturation** — pipe Day -1, bake Day -1, fill Day 0
4. **Sourdough levain needs 2 feeds** — build Wed PM, feed Thu AM, mix Thu PM, retard overnight, bake Fri/Sat AM
5. **Meat pie fillings can be made 2 days ahead** — refrigerate, reheat is not needed (bake from cold)
6. **Baguettes require overnight cold retard** — mix PM, shape PM, retard, bake 3 AM
